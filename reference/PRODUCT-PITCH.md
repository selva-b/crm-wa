# CRM-WA — Product Pitch & Feature Sheet

---

## One-Line Pitch

> **CRM-WA is an all-in-one WhatsApp CRM that helps businesses manage customer conversations, automate follow-ups, run campaigns, track sales, and grow revenue — all from one dashboard.**

---

## The Problem Your Customers Face

Tell your potential customers this:

| Problem | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| Customer messages scattered across personal WhatsApp, email, Instagram | Missed messages = lost sales |
| Only one person can use one WhatsApp number | Team can't collaborate, one person becomes bottleneck |
| No way to track who replied, who didn't, who is interested | Leads fall through the cracks |
| Manual follow-ups — "I forgot to message that customer back" | Lost revenue from forgotten leads |
| No idea which agent is performing well or poorly | Can't improve what you can't measure |
| Sending bulk messages one by one | Hours wasted on repetitive work |
| No customer history — "What did we discuss last time?" | Poor customer experience, repeat questions |
| No automation — everything is manual | High cost, slow response, human errors |

**Bottom line:** Without a CRM, businesses lose customers, waste time, and can't scale.

---

## The Solution — What CRM-WA Does

**CRM-WA connects your WhatsApp Business number to a powerful CRM dashboard. Your entire team can send/receive messages, track leads, run campaigns, automate replies, and see analytics — all from one place.**

Simple setup: Just scan a QR code. No API keys, no developer needed. Ready in 2 minutes.

## Features & Customer Benefits

### 1. WhatsApp Integration — Connect in 2 Minutes

**What it does:**
Connect your WhatsApp Business number by scanning a QR code. Your entire team can use the same number simultaneously.

**Customer benefit:**
- No more "only Ravi has WhatsApp access" problem
- 5 agents can reply from the same WhatsApp number
- All messages are saved — even if an employee leaves, conversations stay

**Example:**
*"Your 3 sales reps can all reply to customers from your main business WhatsApp number. When one is busy, another picks up. No customer waits."*

---

### 2. Multi-Channel Inbox — WhatsApp + Email + Instagram + Facebook

**What it does:**
All customer messages from WhatsApp, Email, Instagram DMs, and Facebook Messenger appear in one unified inbox.

**Customer benefit:**
- No switching between 4 apps
- One agent handles all channels from one screen
- Customer history across all channels in one place

**Example:**
*"A customer messages you on Instagram, then follows up on WhatsApp. Your agent sees both conversations in one place — no context lost."*

---

### 3. AI-Powered Smart Replies & Insights

**What it does:**
- AI suggests replies based on conversation context
- Summarizes long conversations in one click
- Detects customer mood (happy, frustrated, angry)
- Identifies what customer wants (buy, complain, inquire)
- Suggests relevant knowledge base articles

**Customer benefit:**
- Agents reply 3x faster
- New agents handle conversations like experts
- Angry customers get prioritized automatically
- Consistent, high-quality replies

**Example:**
*"A customer asks about pricing. AI instantly suggests the perfect reply with pricing details. Your agent clicks, edits slightly, sends — done in 10 seconds instead of 2 minutes."*

---

### 4. Contact & Lead Management

**What it does:**
- Auto-creates contacts when new customers message you
- Track lead status: New → Contacted → Interested → Qualified → Converted
- Automatic lead scoring (hot leads get higher scores)
- Tags, notes, assignment to specific agents
- Import/export contacts via CSV
- Merge duplicate contacts
- Complete interaction history per contact

**Customer benefit:**
- Never lose a lead
- Know exactly which leads are hot and ready to buy
- Every agent knows the full history before replying
- Clean, organized customer database

**Example:**
*"A new customer messages your WhatsApp. CRM-WA auto-creates their contact, assigns them to your sales team, and starts tracking. Your agent sees 'New Lead — Score: 45' and knows to prioritize them."*

---

### 5. Deals & Sales Pipeline (Kanban Board)

**What it does:**
Visual drag-and-drop board to track sales opportunities through stages: Lead → Qualified → Proposal → Negotiation → Won/Lost.

**Customer benefit:**
- See your entire sales pipeline at a glance
- Know exactly how much revenue is in each stage
- Never forget to follow up on a deal
- Track win/loss rates

**Example:**
*"You have 15 deals worth ₹12 lakhs in 'Proposal' stage and 8 deals worth ₹5 lakhs in 'Negotiation'. You know exactly where to focus your energy."*

---

### 6. Bulk Campaigns — Reach Thousands in One Click

**What it does:**
Send bulk WhatsApp messages to filtered audiences. Schedule for later or send immediately. Track delivery: sent, delivered, read, failed.

**Customer benefit:**
- Send offers/updates to 1000+ customers at once
- Target specific groups (e.g., only VIP customers, only leads from Mumbai)
- Know exactly who read your message
- Schedule campaigns for the best time

**Example:**
*"Diwali offer? Select all customers tagged 'Premium', type your message, schedule for 10 AM — done. 2000 customers get your offer, and you can see 1,800 read it."*

---

### 7. Drip Sequences — Automated Follow-Up Series

**What it does:**
Create automated message sequences that send over days/weeks:
- Day 1: Welcome message
- Day 3: Product introduction
- Day 7: Special offer
- Day 14: Final follow-up

**Customer benefit:**
- Set it and forget it — follow-ups happen automatically
- Nurture leads without manual effort
- Stop automatically when customer replies
- Consistent follow-up for every lead

**Example:**
*"Every new lead gets a 4-message sequence over 2 weeks. You set it up once — it runs forever. Your conversion rate goes from 5% to 15%."*

---

### 8. Automation Rules — If This, Then That

**What it does:**
Create rules like:
- IF customer sends message containing "price" → THEN auto-reply with price list
- IF new contact created → THEN assign to sales team + add "New Lead" tag
- IF no reply for 24 hours → THEN send follow-up message

**Customer benefit:**
- Instant replies even at 2 AM
- No manual sorting or assigning
- Consistent customer experience
- Reduces agent workload by 40-60%

**Example:**
*"A customer messages 'Hi' at midnight. Your automation instantly sends a welcome message, asks what they need, and assigns them to the right team — all before anyone wakes up."*

---

### 9. Chatbot Builder — 24/7 Automated Support

**What it does:**
Visual flow builder to create chatbots. No coding needed. Build question-answer flows with buttons and options.

**Customer benefit:**
- Customers get instant answers 24/7
- Handle FAQs without human agents
- Qualify leads automatically before passing to sales
- Reduce support workload by 50%+

**Example:**
*"Customer messages → Bot asks 'How can I help? 1. Pricing 2. Support 3. Demo' → Customer picks '1' → Bot sends pricing → Customer interested → Bot assigns to human agent."*

---

### 10. CSAT Surveys — Measure Customer Happiness

**What it does:**
Automatically send satisfaction surveys after conversations close. Track scores and trends.

**Customer benefit:**
- Know how happy customers are
- Identify agents who need improvement
- Show management measurable service quality
- Improve based on real feedback

**Example:**
*"After every support conversation, customer gets 'Rate your experience 1-5'. You see your team's average is 4.2/5 — and agent Priya consistently gets 4.8."*

---

### 11. SLA Tracking — Never Miss a Response Deadline

**What it does:**
Set response time policies (e.g., "Reply within 5 minutes for VIP customers"). System alerts when deadlines approach and escalates when breached.

**Customer benefit:**
- Guaranteed response times
- Automatic escalation prevents dropped conversations
- Track compliance rates
- Impress clients with SLA commitments

**Example:**
*"You promise clients a 5-minute response time. CRM-WA alerts your agents at 3 minutes, escalates to manager at 5 minutes. Your SLA compliance: 97%."*

### 12. Knowledge Base — Consistent Answers

**What it does:**
Create a library of articles organized by category. Agents search during conversations. AI suggests relevant articles automatically.

**Customer benefit:**
- Every agent gives the same correct answer
- New agents ramp up in days, not months
- AI surfaces the right article at the right time
- Reduced training time and errors

**Example:**
*"New agent joins your team. Instead of 2 weeks training, they start replying on day 1 — the Knowledge Base and AI guide them with the right answers."*

### 13. Analytics & Reports — Data-Driven Decisions

**What it does:**
Dashboards showing: message volume, response times, conversion funnels, peak activity hours, team performance, campaign results. Export as CSV.

**Customer benefit:**
- See what's working and what's not
- Identify best-performing agents and times
- Track ROI on campaigns
- Reports ready for management reviews

**Example:**
*"Your analytics show peak customer messages at 11 AM and 7 PM. You schedule your campaigns for those times — engagement doubles."*

**Customer benefit:**
- Organized team structure
- Employees only see what they need
- Managers oversee without micromanaging
- Secure — sensitive data protected by permissions

**Example:**
*"Your sales team sees only sales conversations. Support team sees only support. Admin sees everything. No one accidentally deletes campaign data."*
